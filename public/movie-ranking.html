The Best Movies according to Harsha Sunkara

Top 3 Movies of Tollywood

Jersey (Telugu) - 2022

A former cricketer in his late 30s returns to the sport to fulfil his son's dream of owning a cricket jersey, battling personal and professional challenges.

Kalki 2898 AD (Telugu) - 2024

A modern avatar of the Hindu god Vishnu, is said to have descended on Earth to protect the world from evil forces.

RRR (Telugu) - 2022

A fearless revolutionary and an officer in the British force, who once shared a deep bond, decide to join forces and chart out an inspirational path of freedom against the despotic rulers.
